國立中央大學公開徵求管理學院院長候選人啟事

  1. 本校管理學院院長任期將屆滿，特公開徵求具前瞻理念、領導協調能力及國際視野之院長人選。
  2. 任期 3 年，自 115 年 8 月 1 日開始，得續任 1 次。
  3. 應徵者應具下列條件：
    • 具教授資格。
    • 具與本院相關之專長。
    • 具 2 年以上（含）行政主管經驗。
  4. 有意應徵者請於 115 年 5 月 20 日 (星期三) 下班前，將候選人申請表及學歷證件影本等，傳送至 e-mail: chiawan@cc.ncu.edu.tw，或掛號郵寄 32001 桃園市中壢區中大路300號「國立中央大學管理學院院長遴選委員會」林家婉秘書收，聯絡電話：(03)4227151*66068。

NATIONAL CENTRAL UNIVERSITY
Position Announcement: Dean of the School of Management

Term of Office

This position is for a three-year term and is renewable once following the school guidelines. The term will start from August 1, 2026.

Minimum Requirements

  • Having been a university full professor;
  • An active researcher in Management related areas;
  • Having at least 2 years of academic or research administrative experience;
  • Must be fluent in Chinese.

Application Process

All parties interested in applying for the position or nominating a candidate must submit the Application Form for Dean Candidates, Curriculum Vitae, Academic Achievements, and three names for reference before May 20, 2026 by mail or e-mail to:

Mrs. Chia-Wan Lin
School of Management, National Central University
No. 300 Jhongda Rd., Jhongli District, Taoyuan City 32001, Taiwan
E-mail: chiawan@cc.ncu.edu.tw
